The Framework: Research, Plan, Implement

Design your workflow around context management.

1. Research

Understand the codebase, relevant files, data flow, and potential causes.

Use subagents to keep the main context clean.

2. Plan

Outline exact steps, files to edit, and precise testing for each phase.

Human reviews the plan.

3. Implement

Execute phase by phase and compact status back into the plan.

Verify each step before continuing.
